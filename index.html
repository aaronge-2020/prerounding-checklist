      Paste note, labs, and handoff

      Optional. Paste CBC, BMP, cultures, imaging impressions, or trends from the chart. Optional. Paste sign-out summaries, overnight events, to-dos, consultant updates, nursing concerns, or details that are not in the SOAP note. Optional. Paste active medication orders plus MAR administration rows to check whether ordered doses appear to have been given.

      The deidentifier model downloads on first use, then runs locally. Automated de-identification reduces PHI risk but can miss identifiers in notes, lab headers, or handoff text, so review the preview before pasting into OpenEvidence.
