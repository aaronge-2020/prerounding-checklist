      Privacy
      
          
      Local-only privacy guardrails
      
        
      
        
        
          
      • This app does not upload pasted chart text, census data, or case workspaces to an app server by default.
        • 
          
      • No analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or ad scripts are included in this app.
        • 
          
      • Raw pasted chart text is used only for local de-identification in this browser tab, then cleared after review.
        • 
          
      • In census mode, saved de-identified case workspaces and continuity timelines are encrypted in this browser with your local passcode.
        • 
          
      • Review every prompt before copying to OpenEvidence or another external tool. This app reduces PHI risk but does not legally certify HIPAA de-identification; institutional policy, approved tool use, and any required Business Associate Agreement still govern.
        • 
        
      
        
      
          Security posture
          
        
            
      • Default de-identification is structured-only, so starting the app and pasting chart text does not download model assets from third-party CDNs.
        • 
            
      • Enhanced model de-ID is an opt-in aid. It may download code/model assets, and the scrubbed preview still requires manual PHI review.
        • 
            
      • External clinical AI sites are separate systems. Do not paste PHI unless your institution has approved the workflow and required agreements are in place.
